Thank you for scrolling all the way down here! I really appreciate it. Now it's time I tell you something about me. I was born in the Basque country in Spain (as you probably guessed by reading my name), and came to Austria in 2013. I had just finished my Graphic Design studies in San Sebastian, and was all worked up about being in advertising doing creative stuff and I've ever since been working at quite classic Ad Agencies.

During this time I've always been aware of the everchanging market and I never stopped exploring and acquiring new skills such as Graphic Animation, Filming and Coding. It's been a while now, and last year I made a step on what I think is the right direction. I officialy learned Frontend Web Development at Wifi Wien. This helped me work on online projects, build dynamic online banners and be the Digital Allrounder I have always been on the agencies I've worked at. Now I need a new challenge, I need to find a place where my skills are better placed, somewhere with the motto "Digital First".

If I sound to you like a person you would like to work with, please don't hesitate to drop me an e-mail or give me a call!
